## Issues Found

### Issue 1 (MODERATE): Chapter 4-to-5 Transition Gap

**Ch 4 ending:** The group follows Oona toward the fermentation cellars. Marsh follows first, then the others follow Marsh. The two monitoring frogs trail behind. The chapter ends mid-motion, heading to the cellars.

**Ch 5 opening:** The scene begins at the Pools. Quill stands at the edge of the largest pool. No reference to the fermentation cellars, no transitional beat, no indication of elapsed time. The reader must infer that the cellar visit happened offscreen and that some amount of time (likely a day or more, given Quill's internal note "over the past two days") has passed.

**Risk:** The jump is not a contradiction, but "over the past two days" (line 9) implies two days have passed since the humans entered the settlement, not since the cellar visit. This works if the cellar visit was on Day 1 and the Pools gathering is on Day 2 or 3. However, the lack of any acknowledgment of the cellar visit -- even a passing reference like "the fermented drink Oona had given them the night before" -- leaves a seam. The reader who remembers Ch 4's final beat may wonder what happened at the cellars.

**Recommendation:** Add a single clause or sentence early in Ch 5 acknowledging the cellar visit happened. One line is sufficient. Example location: within Quill's early internal observations (around lines 9-11), a phrase like "since Oona had taken them to the fermentation cellars" or a reference to something they experienced there. This stitches the chapters without requiring a scene.

### Issue 2 (MINOR): Vesh's "Twenty-Two Years Teaching" vs. Canon Age

**Canon (characters.md):** Vesh is 22 frog-years old. Described as "mature, past his physical prime, fully in his philosophical prime."

**Ch 5 prose, line 229:** Vesh says: "I have spent twenty-two years teaching it."

**Problem:** If Vesh is 22 frog-years old and has spent 22 years teaching, he has been teaching since birth. This is implausible even by frog standards. A ritual guide would need years of training and maturation before becoming a teacher. The dialogue implies his entire lifespan has been spent teaching, which undermines the canon note that he is "past his physical prime" (suggesting a period of non-teaching physical prime preceded his current state).

**Recommendation:** Change the number in dialogue. Options:
- "I have spent fifteen years teaching it" (implies he began teaching at ~7 frog-years, a reasonable young-adult start).
- "I have spent most of my life teaching it" (avoids a specific number entirely).
- Adjust Vesh's canon age upward if 22 years of teaching is intentional (but this would require updating characters.md and the chapter brief).

### Issue 3 (NEGLIGIBLE): Elderly Frog Gender

**Brief:** "An elderly frog complaining to Vesh that the breathing circle ran too long and made **her** joints stiff."

**Prose:** The elderly frog is described without gendered pronouns ("the elderly frog," "my joints," etc.). No pronoun is used at all.

**Risk:** None. The prose does not contradict the brief; it simply omits the gender marker. This is stylistically consistent with how background frogs are handled throughout. No fix needed unless the brief's "her" is meant to be significant for a later callback.

## Recommendation

**Chapter 5 is approved for manuscript with two requested fixes:**

1. **Add a transitional reference to the fermentation cellars** (from Ch 4's ending) somewhere in the first page of Ch 5. A single clause is sufficient -- e.g., within Quill's "over the past two days" observation, a reference to the cellar visit or something experienced there. This closes the seam between chapters.

2. **Adjust Vesh's "twenty-two years teaching" line** to avoid the implication that he has been teaching since birth. Suggested fix: change to "fifteen years" or replace with "most of my life." If the intent is that Vesh has literally been teaching since infancy (which would be an interesting character detail), this should be made explicit with a supporting sentence rather than left as an apparent arithmetic error.

Both fixes are minor and do not require structural revision. The chapter is otherwise fully canon-compliant, brief-compliant, and constraint-compliant. The ensemble dynamics, voice differentiation, Wet Way integration, and Pruning seeding are all executed at a high level.
